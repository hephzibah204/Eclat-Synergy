BVN Enrolment Services

Secure your banking identity with a Bank Verification Number (BVN). A BVN gives you a unique ID that can be verified across all Nigerian banks, helping to protect your accounts and reduce fraud.

Why BVN?

  • A unique identity recognised across the Nigerian banking industry.
  • Protection against unauthorised account access.
  • Reduces identity theft and exposure to fraud.
  • Accepted as an official form of identification by all Nigerian banks.

How to Enrol

  1. Book an appointment: Call +07 450 444 858 to schedule BVN capturing.
  2. Visit a centre: Walk into any of our enrolment centres for capturing.
  3. Fill the form: Download and complete the BVN enrolment form from our Resources page.
  4. Biometric capture: Present yourself for fingerprint and facial data collection.
  5. Collect your slip: Receive an acknowledgement slip with your transaction ID.
  6. Await confirmation: Within 72 hours the system confirms your application and you receive an SMS to collect your BVN.

For further information about required documentation or step‑by‑step guidance, please contact us or refer to the Resources page.
