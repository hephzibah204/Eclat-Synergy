NIN Enrolment Services

Eclat Synergy offers a complete solution for your Nigerian identity management needs, including NIN enrolment, updates and retrieval. Whether you are enrolling for the first time or updating your details, we’re here to guide you through the process.

Key steps to enrol

  • Call or WhatsApp our booking line at +44 7450 444 858 to schedule your appointment.
  • Visit any of our accredited centres for your biometric capture and document verification.
  • Complete the NIMC pre‑enrolment on penrol.nimc.gov.ng and bring your reference slip.
  • For modifications or updates, use our trusted partner RobostTech.

Additional Services

  • NIN Modification: Redirects you to our trusted partner for modifications and updates via the RobostTech API.
  • NIMC Pre‑Enrolment: Complete your pre‑enrolment on the official site penrol.nimc.gov.ng.
  • NPC Birth Certificate / Attestation of Birth: For assistance obtaining your certificate or attestation, please contact customer service on +234 703 253 2424.

If you have questions about eligibility, required documents or the status of your registration, please visit our Resources page or contact us.
