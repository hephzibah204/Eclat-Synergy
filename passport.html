Nigerian Passport Processing

Eclat Synergy assists Nigerian citizens in the diaspora with obtaining and renewing Nigerian passports, as well as registering businesses at the Corporate Affairs Commission and fulfilling compliance requirements.

Passport Services

  • New passport applications: We guide you through the application, ensure all documents are prepared correctly and help schedule your appointment.
  • Renewals and replacements: Assistance with renewing expired passports or replacing lost/damaged passports.
  • Document preparation: Advice on required documents, fee schedules and current processing times.

Business Registration & Compliance

We also facilitate business registrations in Nigeria and compliance filings with the Corporate Affairs Commission. Our services include company name searches, incorporation filing, post‑incorporation changes and statutory compliance updates.
