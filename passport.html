Nigerian Passport Processing

Important note

  • Eclat Synergy provides guidance and application assistance — passport issuance is handled by the Nigerian Immigration Service (NIS).
  • We help you prepare a complete, correct application so there are no delays or rejections.

Obtaining or renewing a Nigerian international passport from abroad can be complex and time-consuming. Our experienced team walks you through every requirement, helps you gather the right documents, and liaises with the appropriate authorities to give your application the best chance of success.

Documents Required

  • ✓ Completed online NIS passport application form (we assist with this)
  • ✓ Valid NIN slip or NIN card
  • ✓ Birth certificate or age declaration
  • ✓ Current passport (for renewals) or affidavit of loss if lost
  • ✓ 2 recent passport photographs (white background, 35×45mm)
  • ✓ Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
  • ✓ Proof of UK residence (BRP, visa, or equivalent)

Application Steps

  1. Initial consultation — Contact us so we can review your situation and advise on requirements.
  2. Document preparation — We help you gather and verify all required documents.
  3. Online application — We assist you in completing the NIS online application correctly.
  4. Payment of NIS fees — Official fees paid directly to NIS (we advise on current amounts).
  5. Appointment booking — We help you book your biometric appointment at the relevant Nigerian High Commission or Consulate.
  6. Follow-up — We track your application status and alert you when your passport is ready for collection.

FAQs
