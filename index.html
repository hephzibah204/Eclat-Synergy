About Us

Eclat Synergy specialises in supporting Nigerians in the diaspora. Through our sister company FUWA Agribusiness we are licensed to provide National Identity Number (NIN) enrolment services. We also assist with Nigerian passport issuance and business registration, operate fulfilment services for deliveries, run car/van rentals through our Drivalia franchise and offer document notarisation.

Our Story

Eclat Synergy was founded to empower Nigerians in the diaspora. We work with official agencies to provide identity enrolment, documentation assistance and trusted logistics services. Read about our mission, values and vision in our detailed About Us page.
