Dispatch and Distribution Manager

Job Title: Dispatch and Distribution Manager

Job Code: 1241 Managers in Transport and Distribution

Job Type: Full-time, Permanent

Location: Aberdeen, United Kingdom

Salary: £44,900–£50,000 per annum (depending on experience)

Hours: 37.5–40 hours per week

About the Role

We are seeking an experienced Dispatch and Distribution Manager to lead our dispatch and distribution operations. The successful candidate will oversee the movement of goods from warehouse to customers, ensuring deliveries are completed safely, efficiently, and on schedule while maintaining compliance with UK transport and health and safety regulations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Plan, organise and manage daily dispatch and distribution activities.
  • Supervise dispatch supervisors, warehouse staff and delivery drivers.
  • Ensure the accurate picking, packing and timely dispatch of customer orders.
  • Manage transport schedules and optimise delivery routes.
  • Monitor inventory movement and coordinate with warehouse operations.
  • Develop and implement distribution strategies to improve efficiency and reduce costs.
  • Ensure compliance with transport legislation, health and safety regulations, and company policies.
  • Manage fleet utilisation, maintenance schedules and transport documentation.
  • Investigate delivery issues and implement corrective actions.
  • Prepare operational reports, KPIs and departmental budgets.

Essential Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Logistics, Supply Chain Management, Business Management, or a related discipline, or equivalent industry experience.
  • Minimum of 3 years’ experience in dispatch, distribution or logistics management.
  • Strong leadership and team management skills.
  • Excellent planning, organisational and communication abilities.
  • Knowledge of UK transport regulations and warehouse operations.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office and warehouse/logistics management systems.
  • Clean Drivers license (Manual preferred)

Desirable

  • Certificate of Professional Competence (CPC).
  • Experience managing multi-site distribution operations.
  • Knowledge of ERP or Warehouse Management Systems (WMS).

Benefits

  • Competitive salary
  • Company pension
  • Annual performance bonus
  • 28 days annual leave (including bank holidays)
  • Ongoing training and career development
  • Employee Assistance Programme

How to Apply

  • An up-to-date CV
  • A cover letter outlining their relevant experience
