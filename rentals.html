Car & Van Rentals

Eclat Synergy operates a franchise with Drivalia providing car hire services for residents in Dundee and surrounding areas.

Whether you need a small car for city travel or a van for moving goods, our fleet has a variety of vehicles to meet your needs. Flexible rental periods and competitive rates ensure you only pay for what you need.

Why choose us?

  • Wide range of vehicles from compact cars to large vans.
  • Flexible rental durations – hourly, daily, weekly or longer.
  • Insurance and roadside assistance included.

For reservations and vehicle availability, please contact us or call our customer service.
