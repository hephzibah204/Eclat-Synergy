Overview

Key takeaways

  • Eclat Synergy offers NIN & BVN enrolment, notarisation, passport assistance and logistics for Nigerians abroad.
  • Through our partnership with FUWA Agribusiness, we are licensed to register and update National Identity Numbers.
  • Our centres across Scotland, England, Wales and Germany mean we're always close to where you are.

Eclat Synergy was founded to simplify identity and administrative processes for Nigerians living overseas. Navigating government services can be challenging when you're far from home — our goal is to remove those barriers. We work closely with official agencies to ensure your NIN and BVN enrolment goes smoothly. We also provide notarisation services, last-mile fulfilment via Post & Packing UK, car and van rentals through our Drivalia franchise, and guidance for obtaining Nigerian passports and business registration.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Nigerians living abroad by providing accessible, reliable and efficient identity management and support services. We believe that every Nigerian should be able to exercise their rights, access opportunities and stay connected to home — regardless of where they live.

We're committed to outstanding customer service and maintaining the highest standards of compliance and data security. Our staff are trained to handle sensitive personal information responsibly and to guide you through every step of your enrolment or application.

Our Values

🤝 Integrity

We operate transparently and abide by all regulatory requirements in every country we serve.

💛 Empathy

We recognise the challenges of navigating bureaucracy from abroad and strive to make every interaction supportive.

⚡ Innovation

♿ Accessibility

Services Snapshot

Eclat Synergy operates several business units to meet diverse needs:

  • NIN Enrolment: Register or update your National Identity Number through our licensed centres.
  • BVN Enrolment: Secure your banking identity with a Bank Verification Number.
  • Passport Processing: Guidance and assistance for obtaining or renewing your Nigerian international passport.
  • Notarisation: Get your documents notarised by approved officials for UK or Nigerian use.
  • Mailbox Rental: A secure private mailing address for personal or business correspondence.
  • Fulfilment Services: Receive, store, pack and dispatch parcels through our Post & Packing UK partnership.
  • Post Worldwide: Send items to Nigeria or anywhere in the world at competitive rates.
  • Vehicle Rentals: Hire cars and vans via our Drivalia partnership in Dundee and other locations.

Looking Ahead

We are continuously expanding our network of centres and exploring new services to better serve our clients. Our long-term vision is to be the trusted hub for Nigerians abroad seeking identity management, documentation, logistics and mobility solutions.

We welcome feedback from our customers and stakeholders. If you have suggestions or questions, please contact us. Your input helps us improve and develop new offerings tailored to your needs.
