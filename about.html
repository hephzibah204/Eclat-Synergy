Overview

Key takeaways

  • Eclat Synergy offers identity enrolment (NIN and BVN), document notarisation, passport assistance and logistics services for Nigerians abroad.
  • Through our partnership with FUWA Agribusiness, we are licensed to register and update National Identity Numbers.
  • Our fulfilment centres in the UK, Wales and Germany make it easy to access services close to you.

Eclat Synergy was founded to simplify identity and administrative processes for Nigerians living overseas. Navigating government services can be challenging when you’re far from home; our goal is to remove those barriers. We work closely with official agencies to ensure your National Identity Number (NIN) and Bank Verification Number (BVN) enrolment goes smoothly. We also provide notarisation services, last‑mile fulfilment via Post & Packing UK, car and van rentals, and guidance for obtaining Nigerian passports and business registration. With us, you can take care of multiple administrative tasks in one place.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Nigerians living abroad by providing accessible, reliable and efficient identity management and support services. We believe that every Nigerian should be able to exercise their rights, access opportunities and stay connected to home regardless of where they live. By offering a suite of services under one roof, we save you time and reduce the stress of dealing with multiple agencies across different time zones.

We’re committed to delivering outstanding customer service and maintaining the highest standards of compliance and data security. Our staff are trained to handle sensitive personal information responsibly and to guide you through each step of your enrolment or application.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We operate transparently and abide by all regulatory requirements.
  • Empathy: We recognise the challenges of navigating bureaucracy from abroad and strive to make every interaction supportive.
  • Innovation: We embrace digital tools and processes to offer more efficient and engaging services.
  • Accessibility: We are committed to delivering inclusive services for people of all abilities, with clear communication and accessible design.

Services Snapshot

Eclat Synergy operates several business units to meet diverse needs:

  • NIN Enrolment: Register for or update your National Identity Number through our licensed centres.
  • BVN Enrolment: Secure your banking identity with a Bank Verification Number.
  • Mail Box Rental: Keep your correspondence safe with a personal or business mailbox.
  • Fulfilment Services: Drop off, pick up and ship packages through our Post & Packing franchise.
  • Notarisation: Get your documents notarised by approved officials.
  • Passport Processing: Receive guidance and assistance for obtaining or renewing your Nigerian passport.
  • Vehicle Rentals: Hire cars and vans via our Drivalia partnership in Dundee and other locations.

Visit the service pages from the navigation menu for detailed steps and requirements. Each page includes instructions, contact details and booking options.

Our long‑term vision is to be the trusted hub for Nigerians abroad seeking identity management, documentation, logistics and mobility solutions.
