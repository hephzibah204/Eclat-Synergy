Nigerian NIN Enrolment Services in the UK

Eclat Synergy Limited is proud to support the Nigerian diaspora by providing National Identification Number (NIN) enrolment and biometric data capture services on behalf of the National Identity Management Commission (NIMC), Nigeria.

We operate NIN enrolment centres across the United Kingdom, including:

  • Aberdeen
  • Glasgow
  • Edinburgh
  • Birmingham
  • Hull
  • Derby
  • Swansea
  • Milton Keynes
  • Hemel Hempstead

Our experienced team assists Nigerians living in the UK with secure biometric data capture and NIN registration, making the enrolment process convenient and accessible.
